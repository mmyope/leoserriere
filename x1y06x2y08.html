Posters for MMYOPE

posters designed for the various books done thus far. we wanted to make something that would reveal, highlight, or uncover, pieces of the books that were otherwise veiled, obscured, buried.

part of this process was also to print recto-verso, not only for the bargain — but also because we wanted to keep one side the same for each separate poster of each book. meaning, one side behaves like an anchor, and the other the design.

the choice of display is left to the owner.
